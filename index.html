Steve Rubin

The basics

I am a second-year CS Ph.D. student at Berkeley, fresh out of Williams College where I majored in both Computer Science and Mathematics. I'm advised by Maneesh Agrawala. At Williams I served as president and root of Williams Students Online, competed on and co-captained the track and field team, and wrote a senior thesis with Duane Bailey.

Research interests

As a young, impressionable grad student, I'm not sure about my specific research interests. I can, however, say that I'm interested in information visualization, HCI, music, and, to a lesser extent, systems. If you have any ideas for awesome projects, persuade me, please!

My undergraduate senior thesis developed a methodology for visualizing large, highly symmetric communication graphs, such as process communication graphs from parallel programs.
